heartbeats

In this experiment, you will classify heartbeats as either regular or irregular. You will listen to a heartbeat recording, then respond by pressing r for regular or i for irregular. Each recording will play for 8 seconds, followed by 2 second response window. You may respond during either. There will be two sets of trials, one where you are provided with feedback and one without feedback. You are to ignore any miscellaneous noises that may be heard during the recordings.

By pressing the button below, you consent to your participation in this experiment. You must use headphones for this experiment as the heartbeat sounds are inaudible through speakers. If you have any questions, please contact the experimenter.
