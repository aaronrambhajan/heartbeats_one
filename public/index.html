heartbeats

In this experiment, you will classify heartbeats as either regular or irregular. You will listen to a heartbeat recording, and are to respond by pressing r for regular or i for irregular. You must respond before the recording finishes. The recordings are approximately 10 seconds long. You will receive feedback fo the first half, and no feedback for the second half.

By pressing the button below, you consent to your participation in this experiment. This experiment must be done using headphones. If you have any questions, please contact the experimenter.
